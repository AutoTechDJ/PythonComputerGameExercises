How to Play Dragon Realm

in this game, the player is in a land full of dragons. the dragons all live in caves with their large piles of collected treasurre. some dragons are
friendly and share their treasure. Other dragons are hungry and eat anyone who enters their cave. The player approaches two caves, one with friendly
dragon and the other with hungry dragon, but doesn't know which dragon is in which cave. The player must choose betweeen the two.
